REDUCE REUSE RECYCLE
 

The 3 R's of sustainability, Reduce, Reuse, and Recycle, are key principles of sustainable waste management. They encourage us to reduce waste by consuming less and conserving resources, find ways to reuse items instead of throwing them away, and collect and process waste materials to create new products. Together, they are an effective way to minimize waste and promote sustainability.
